About

Matthew Russell LaBarre is a multi‑instrumentalist, sound healer and cosmic explorer based in Binghamton, New York. Performing under the aliases Victor, W Vector and Hansraj Chand, his work blends vibroacoustic textures, binaural beats and ancient frequencies to guide listeners into meditative states. LaBarre operates under the Planetary Tones label, a project rooted in experimental, binaural and brainwave music tagged with terms such as “vibroacoustic,” “isochronic” and “Binghamton.” Planetary Tones releases include long‑form meditations like Abundance Alignment (released 18 August 2023), 528hz Om Rain Meditation with Schumann Resonance (released 25 September 2022) and Sacred Temple Sonic Space‑Time (released 26 December 2021). These pieces harness celestial frequencies—Jupiter binaurals, sacred cubit bells, Schumann resonance and theta waves—to facilitate healing and alignment. When he isn’t recording, LaBarre shares cosmic insights through social media as @matthewlabarre on Instagram and X.
